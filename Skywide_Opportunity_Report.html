Part 3 — Gap Analysis: What SEO Brand Does That Skywide Doesn’t Support

🔴 Critical Gaps

GAP-01: No GEO / AI Search Optimization Tracking

SEO Brand’s #1 current growth vector is AI SEO / GEO — optimizing for ChatGPT, Claude, Google AI Overviews. Their most recent blog posts are about it. They have a named service line for it. A case study shows “12 AI Overview placements” for a client. A recent hire is “Director of SEO & GEO.”

Skywide has zero tooling for this. There is no way to: - Track whether a client appears in ChatGPT or Claude responses - Monitor AI Overview appearances in Google - Generate GEO-optimized content vs. standard SEO content - Measure “LLM visibility” which they’re now reporting in case studies

This is the #1 gap. It’s their flagship new service and Skywide doesn’t touch it.

GAP-02: Research Feature Produces No Output

The /research page exists, accepts a URL, and sends it to /api/proxy-research — but the UI never renders any results. The endpoint likely forwards to N8N for competitive analysis. Whatever comes back is discarded. Staff are unable to use a feature that’s listed in their navigation.

Fix: Fully implement the Research output UI. This is a medium-effort build that would unlock competitive analysis directly in Skywide.

GAP-03: No PPC / Paid Media Support

SEO Brand runs Google Ads, Meta Ads, Amazon PPC, LinkedIn Ads, YouTube Ads, and Enterprise PPC. They have a dedicated Media Buyer and Paid Media Specialist on the team. They are actively hiring a Google Ads Media Buyer.

Skywide is 100% content/SEO focused. There is no: - Ad copy generation (different rules from SEO content) - PPC brief generation - Campaign naming or structure tooling - Ad performance tracking - Audience targeting documentation

GAP-04: No Client Management UI

Adding a new indexing client, onboarding a new content client, or deactivating a client all require direct Supabase database access. There is no admin UI for: - Adding/editing/deactivating clients - Configuring client workbook URLs and GSC properties - Assigning clients to account managers - Client-level settings

Every client addition is a developer task. With 5 indexing clients and more coming, this is already a bottleneck.

GAP-05: No Rank Tracking / SERP Integration

SEO Brand’s core deliverable is rankings. Their proposals promise “100,000+ pages ranked on Google.” Their monthly reports show rank improvements. Skywide has no integration with rank tracking data. There’s no way to: - See where a client ranks for their target keywords - Compare before/after adding content - Track ranking gains as a content ROI metric - Connect the dots between a content piece and a ranking improvement

🟡 High-Value Gaps

GAP-06: No Content Calendar / Pipeline View

Content requests are submitted ad hoc. There’s no calendar to see what’s scheduled, what’s in progress per client, or team workload distribution. As they scale Skywide usage across the team, this becomes critical.

GAP-07: No Link Building Tracker

Link building is explicitly listed as a core service. They have a full process: digital PR, guest posting, competitor backlink analysis, contextual anchor text strategy. None of this has a Skywide workflow. Outreach tracking, placement logging, DA scoring, and anchor text documentation happen entirely outside the platform.

GAP-08: Quality Score Not Surfaced

The database has a quality_score column (migration Jan 2026). The N8N pipeline computes it. It’s never displayed anywhere in the UI. Staff have no way to filter or sort content by quality score.

GAP-09: No Error Alerting

When indexing cron jobs fail, or content runs error out, staff only discover it by checking the dashboard. No Slack webhook, no email, no in-app notification. Silent failures are dangerous at scale.

GAP-10: AI Rewriter Has No Export

Staff refine content using the AI Rewriter but can’t export it — no copy-to-clipboard with formatting preserved, no Google Docs push, no download. This creates a manual copy-paste step for every piece of content.

GAP-11: No E-E-A-T / CRO Checklist Tooling

SEO Brand publicly markets “Google E-E-A-T Compliance” as a Skywide feature. While the N8N pipeline may run an EEAT pass, there’s no visible checklist, scoring UI, or structured report showing which E-E-A-T signals were addressed. Similarly, they offer CRO — but there’s no content-level CRO audit or UX feedback in Skywide.

GAP-12: GSC Performance Data Pull

The indexing system already has a Google Apps Script that authenticates against GSC. Extending this to pull Performance data (clicks, impressions, CTR, position) per client would enable: - Showing whether submitted URLs are actually getting impressions - Tracking click-through rate improvements after content updates - Measuring the ROI of the indexing automation in ranking terms

🟢 Strategic / Growth Gaps

GAP-13: No Client-Facing Portal

All Skywide features are internal-only. SEO Brand clients cannot log in to see their content pipeline, indexing status, or performance. A read-only client view would add significant perceived value, differentiate from competitors, and reduce status-update emails.

GAP-14: No PDF Report Generator

Agencies live by monthly client reports. There’s no automated report generation in Skywide. A report covering “X URLs indexed, Y content pieces published, Z ranking improvements” would save hours per client per month and create a professional artifact for client retention.

GAP-15: Reputation Management Tooling

SEO Brand explicitly offers Online Reputation Management. There’s no Skywide feature for review monitoring, sentiment tracking, review response drafting, or ORM reporting.
