=== SKYWIDE CONTENT ENGINE: MASTER QA CHECKLIST ===
Use this checklist to programmatically and manually evaluate all AI-generated content outputs against the historical bugs and requirements gathered since March 2026.

[ ] 1. TRUNCATION & COMPLETENESS CHECK
- Does the article end with a complete sentence and proper punctuation?
- Are the FAQs and Conclusion sections fully present?
- Did the article hit an artificial token ceiling and cut off abruptly?

[ ] 2. STRUCTURAL COMPLIANCE (THE BRIEF)
- Are all exact H2s requested in the brief present?
- Did the AI follow negative constraints specified in the brief (e.g., "Do not use em-dashes", "Always mention the city")?

[ ] 3. REDUNDANCY & REPETITION CHECK
- Structural Redundancy: Does any H2 directly duplicate the phrasing of the H1/Title?
- Verbal Redundancy: Does the AI repeat the same transitional phrases (e.g., "Additionally", "Moreover") excessively?

[ ] 4. PERCENTAGE & STATISTICAL HALLUCINATION
- Does the article contain unverified percentage statistics (e.g., "78% of patients report...", "Over 60% of cases...")?
- If statistics are present, are they strictly traced back to the Client Website or the Creative Brief?

[ ] 5. AUTHORITY & PRACTITIONER HALLUCINATION
- Does the article cite vague, unnamed practitioners (e.g., "Clinical experience shows", "Practitioners consistently see")?
- Does the article invent guidelines or endorsements from real organizations (e.g., "The American Psychological Association recommends") unless explicitly provided in the brief?

[ ] 6. CLIENT GROUND-TRUTH & SERVICE DRIFT
- Does the article claim the client offers a specific service, product, or treatment that is NOT present on the client's actual website?
- Is the client name mentioned naturally, especially within the first paragraph?

[ ] 7. CONTEXT CONTAMINATION
- Does the core subject matter align 100% with the brief? (Verify that the AI did not hallucinate a completely different topic, e.g., writing about "Teen Anxiety" when the brief asked for "PE Ethics").

[ ] 8. FORMATTING & READABILITY
- Is the passive voice minimized?
- Are paragraphs kept concise (under 4-5 sentences) for readability?
- Does the introduction have a compelling hook without sounding overly AI-generated?

=== END OF CHECKLIST ===
